# CIC P vs NP Research Master Plan

## Overview
This project investigates the P vs NP problem through the lens of Computational Information Complexity (CIC).

## Research Tracks
- **Track A**: Circuit complexity and information
- **Track B**: Proof complexity bounds
- **Track C**: Algorithmic approaches
- **Track D**: Experimental validation
- **Track E**: Barrier analysis

## Stages
1. Literature review and framework development
2. Theoretical analysis and theorem proving
3. Experimental design and implementation
4. Results analysis and synthesis
5. Paper writing and documentation

## Timeline
Approximately 12 stages of focused research.
