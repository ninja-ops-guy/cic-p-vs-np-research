# Track W: P vs PC (Polynomial vs Probabilistic Circuit) Gap

## Overview
Analysis of the gap between polynomial-time computation and probabilistic circuit complexity.

## Definitions
- **PC**: The class of problems solvable by polynomial-size probabilistic circuits
- **Gap**: The difference in power between P and PC

## Key Question
Is P = PC? If not, what is the relationship?

## Results
1. P ⊆ PC (trivial)
2. If P = NP, then P = PC
3. Under standard derandomization assumptions, P = BPP ⊆ PC

## Implications for P vs NP
- If P != PC, then P != NP
- The P vs PC question may be easier to resolve
- Derandomization results provide partial answers
