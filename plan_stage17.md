- **Track MMM** (Formalization): 5 theorems proved — ETC is solver-independent, O(w·n) for treewidth w, Ω(n) for PHP, bounded by resolution width
- **Track NNN** (Scaling): Framework sound but entropy definition needs refinement (H=0 for UNSAT formulas)
