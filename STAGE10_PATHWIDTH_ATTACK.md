# Stage 10: Pathwidth Attack Analysis

## Overview
Investigation of pathwidth as a complexity measure and its implications for SAT solving.

## Key Results
- Pathwidth provides tighter bounds than treewidth for certain formula classes
- SAT is fixed-parameter tractable parameterized by pathwidth
- Industrial instances often have low pathwidth

## Approach
- Pathwidth decomposition algorithms
- SAT solving via dynamic programming on path decompositions
- Comparison with treewidth-based approaches

## Implications
Pathwidth-based attacks may yield new algorithmic insights for structured SAT instances.
