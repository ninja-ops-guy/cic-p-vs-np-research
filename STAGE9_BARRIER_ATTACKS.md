# Stage 9: Barrier Attacks Analysis

## Overview
Analysis of known barriers to resolving P vs NP and strategies to circumvent them.

## The Three Barriers
1. **Relativization**: There exist oracles A,B such that P^A = NP^A and P^B != NP^B
2. **Natural Proofs**: Any sufficiently general circuit lower bound technique would break pseudorandom generators
3. **Algebrization**: Extension of relativization to algebraic oracles

## Strategies
- Focus on non-relativizing techniques
- Avoid Natural Proofs barrier through information-theoretic approach
- Develop barrier-specific proof techniques

## Conclusion
The CIC framework's information-theoretic approach may circumvent the Natural Proofs barrier by not directly applying to circuit size lower bounds.
