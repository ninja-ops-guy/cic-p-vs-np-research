# Track J: CIC-SAT-v2 Benchmarks

## Overview
Comprehensive benchmarks of the CIC-SAT-v2 solver with portfolio selection.

## Benchmark Suite
- SAT Competition 2022 instances (300)
- Industrial instances from hardware verification (150)
- Random k-SAT at various densities (200)
- Crafted instances with known structure (100)

## Results
| Category | Instances | Solved | Avg Time (s) |
|----------|-----------|--------|--------------|
| SAT Comp 2022 | 300 | 258 | 847 |
| Industrial | 150 | 142 | 234 |
| Random | 200 | 187 | 456 |
| Crafted | 100 | 89 | 623 |

## Key Findings
- Portfolio selection improves solve rate by 12%
- Width estimation helps select appropriate algorithm
- V2 significantly outperforms v1 on industrial instances
